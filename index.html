About us

Improving Communities

At Parkhaven, we create communities where residents are safe and happy, regardless of their budget. We strive to provide a wonderful place to live with the amenities that matter. We take feedback from our residents seriously and see ourselves as partners in the community, helping to create a better tomorrow.

The Problem

Manufactured home communities have typically been owned and operated by aging mom-and-pop operators. These operators frequently started with a unit or two and over time found themselves managing parks with little experience and little to no capital for improvements and maintenance. This system caused manufactured homes to be viewed as less than desirable by the residents and City.

Our Solution

We have found that our best communities are communities where our residents own their homes.
